About Us

Ajith Enterprises is a well-established Tool Room and Precision Injection Moulding unit located at Hebbal Industrial Area, Mysore. Founded in 2005 by Mr. Ajit Kini — a Tool & Die maker trained at NTTF — we deliver high-quality plastic components to reputed industrial and defence clients with a strong record of precision, reliability, and on-time delivery.
